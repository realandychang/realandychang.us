Story

Andy Chang is currently the owner of Andy Chang Team. When he was 6 years old, he really like technology products. He usually look at other people's ideas and combine all of the ideas together. There are a lot of websites that inspired him and he decided to follow what they did and made some change to it.

Before he found Andy Chang Team, he couldn't comprehend english well. Andy Chang migrated to United States in the year 2018, and started school at that year. He started to learn English and practice English when he got time. As a result, English almost became his first language.
